Games

The free web game “In for a Loop” is being developed in response to the SEND Norfolk Local Offer. This game is being developed for 10 to 15 year olds with SEND and aims to be published in late June for public consumption. The developer and resources for this project are being provided by the College of West Anglia.
×
In for a Loop is a free 2D online game where the player must build a rollercoaster fun enough to pass the level but not so fun, or dangerous that the riders die in transit. You will have access to a variety of fun loops, twists and props to liven up the carriage ride just be careful not to put too many intense parts next to each other lest the passengers suffer the -age appropriate- consequences.
